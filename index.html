About Me

From conducting experimental research, I’ve become motivated to deliver data-driven insights to curate compelling data stories and a data-first culture. In particular, I see an overarching goal between software engineering, computational science and psychology. The goal being to empathises with user capabilities and limitations, and create their own personalised data journey.

Now equipped with the essential data engineering and data analytics knowledge, I am enthused about ideating, conceptualizing and producing consumer-centric as well as technology-enabled products from level-zero. I look to use Big Data obtained from social networks and smart wearables to ensure success across the commercial and consumer marketing functions, deeply ingrained in data analytics and delivery of revenue marketing strategies.

Fully aware of the software development life cycle, I look to thrive in data roles to which will stimulate me to curate data driven insights to influence commercial and consumer marketing functions.
